# Community Service Website (CWM)

CWM is a platform designed to connect communities, empower youth, and drive meaningful social change through various initiatives and programs.

---

## Features
- **Community Projects**: Showcase initiatives like agriculture training, coding workshops, and social advocacy programs.  
- **Volunteer Opportunities**: Find ways to contribute to impactful causes.  
- **Social Media Integration**: Stay connected through our official social platforms.

---

## How to Use
1. Visit the website to explore projects and events.  
2. Get involved by signing up for volunteer opportunities.  
3. Share your feedback to help us grow.

---

## Technologies Used
- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript  
- **Backend**: Node.js (or relevant backend stack)
